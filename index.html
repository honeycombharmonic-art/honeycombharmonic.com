The work

The body learns through experience.

Somatic Reeducation introduces precise sensory information without forcing an outcome. The work can look unusual because it follows function, not convention.

Working principle

The nested pain model

Pain is never only physical.

We do not treat these as isolated buckets. Embodiment is the ongoing organization of body, feeling, relationship, and meaning.
