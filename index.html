The centerline

A body is not a machine. It is a living system that responds to better evidence.

The work here is built around steady attention, clear pressure, honest pacing, and products that feel good under real hands. No mystic fog. No sterile clinic costume. Just depth, beauty, and useful contact.

Sessions, balms, and classes share one aim: help the body find options other than bracing, collapse, or pushing through.
